Curry

Description:

This is the most delicous curry ever. And its calorie friendly

Ingridients

  • 300g chicken breast
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Tumiric
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Curry
  • Cinnamon (Optional)

Guide

  1. chop garlic,ginger
  2. cut chicken into bite sized pieces
  3. Add olive oil to the pan and cook ginger and garlic until it looks kind like glass
  4. Add chicken cubes
  5. Cook until cooked
  6. add coconut milk
  7. cook for 2 mins
  8. add tomato paste, salt, curry, cinnamon and tumiric
  9. Cook under the lit for 15minutes
  10. Serve with rice
